Sexual Diseases

1. Chlamydia

Symptoms: Abnormal discharge, burning sensation during urination, pain during intercourse.

Causes: Bacterial infection caused by Chlamydia trachomatis.

Treatment: Antibiotics prescribed by a healthcare provider.

2. Gonorrhea

Symptoms: Painful urination, pus-like discharge, sore throat (if oral contact).

Causes: Bacterial infection caused by Neisseria gonorrhoeae.

Treatment: Antibiotics, usually a combination of medications.

3. Syphilis

Symptoms: Sores (chancre), rash, mucous membrane lesions, flu-like symptoms.

Causes: Bacterial infection caused by Treponema pallidum.

Treatment: Penicillin or other antibiotics as prescribed.

4. Herpes Simplex Virus (HSV)

Symptoms: Painful sores or blisters around the genital area, itching, and burning.

Causes: Viral infection caused by HSV-1 or HSV-2.

Treatment: Antiviral medications to reduce symptoms and frequency of outbreaks.

5. Human Papillomavirus (HPV)

Symptoms: Genital warts, itching, sometimes no visible symptoms.

Causes: Viral infection caused by human papillomavirus.

Treatment: Warts may be treated with topical medications or removed by a healthcare provider.

6. Trichomoniasis

Symptoms: Itching, discharge, discomfort during urination or sex.

Causes: Parasitic infection caused by Trichomonas vaginalis.

Treatment: Antibiotics, usually metronidazole or tinidazole.

7. Hepatitis B

Symptoms: Jaundice, abdominal pain, fatigue, nausea.

Causes: Viral infection caused by Hepatitis B virus (HBV).

Treatment: Antiviral medications, and in some cases, liver transplant.

8. Hepatitis C

Symptoms: Fatigue, jaundice, abdominal pain, nausea.

Causes: Viral infection caused by Hepatitis C virus (HCV).

Treatment: Antiviral medications, often a combination of drugs.

9. Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV)

Symptoms: Flu-like symptoms, weight loss, chronic diarrhea.

Causes: Viral infection caused by HIV.

Treatment: Antiretroviral therapy (ART) to manage symptoms and prolong life.

10. Bacterial Vaginosis (BV)

Symptoms: Abnormal discharge, fishy odor, itching.

Causes: Imbalance of bacteria in the vagina.

Treatment: Antibiotics, such as metronidazole or clindamycin.
