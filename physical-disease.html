Physical Diseases

1. Flu

Symptoms: Fever, chills, cough, sore throat, runny or stuffy nose, muscle or body aches, headache, fatigue.

Causes: Influenza viruses.

Treatment: Rest, fluids, over-the-counter medications, antiviral drugs in severe cases.

2. Cold

Symptoms: Runny nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, congestion.

Causes: Rhinoviruses.

Treatment: Rest, fluids, over-the-counter medications for symptom relief.

3. Asthma

Symptoms: Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, coughing.

Causes: Airway inflammation and hyperreactivity.

Treatment: Inhaled corticosteroids, bronchodilators, avoiding triggers.

4. Diabetes Mellitus

Symptoms: Excessive thirst, frequent urination, extreme fatigue, blurred vision.

Causes: Insulin resistance (Type 2) or autoimmune destruction of insulin-producing cells (Type 1).

Treatment: Insulin therapy, oral medications, lifestyle changes, monitoring blood sugar levels.

5. Hypertension

Symptoms: Often asymptomatic; severe cases may include headaches, shortness of breath, nosebleeds.

Causes: Lifestyle factors, genetic predisposition, kidney disease.

Treatment: Lifestyle changes, antihypertensive medications, regular monitoring.

6. Osteoarthritis

Symptoms: Joint pain, stiffness, swelling, decreased range of motion.

Causes: Joint wear and tear, genetic factors, previous joint injuries.

Treatment: Pain management, physical therapy, joint injections, and in severe cases, surgery.

7. Rheumatoid Arthritis

Symptoms: Joint pain, morning stiffness, joint swelling, fatigue.

Causes: Autoimmune disorder where the immune system attacks the joints.

Treatment: Disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs (DMARDs), pain relief medications, physical therapy.

8. Migraine

Symptoms: Severe headache, nausea, sensitivity to light and sound.

Causes: Genetic factors, hormonal changes, environmental triggers.

Treatment: Pain relief medications, lifestyle changes, avoiding triggers.

9. Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD)

Symptoms: Heartburn, regurgitation, chest pain, difficulty swallowing.

Causes: Weakness of the lower esophageal sphincter, acid reflux from the stomach.

Treatment: Antacids, H2-receptor antagonists, proton pump inhibitors, dietary changes.

10. Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, chronic cough, wheezing, mucus production.

Causes: Long-term exposure to irritating gases or particulate matter, smoking being the primary cause.

Treatment: Smoking cessation, bronchodilators, corticosteroids, oxygen therapy.
